Mega Infracon | Quality You Can Trust

Mega Infracon is committed to delivering premium residential and commercial spaces with a strong focus on quality, functionality, and modern amenities. Each project is thoughtfully designed to offer comfort, durability, and long-term value, while maintaining an eco-conscious approach to construction.

Founded in 2023 and headquartered in Vijayawada, Mega Infracon has rapidly emerged as a trusted name in the construction sector. Under the leadership of Polepalli A. V. Subbarao, the company has successfully completed and continues to execute a total of 5 projects, showcasing its dedication to timely delivery and quality execution.
