Mega Infracon Begins Construction of 30,000 Sq. Ft. FMCG Factory in Hyderabad

Mega Infracon Construction Company has officially started work on a 30,000 sq. ft. FMCG factory in Hyderabad. The project is designed to meet modern manufacturing standards, focusing on efficiency, safety, and scalability to support fast-moving consumer goods production.

The FMCG factory project in Hyderabad marks another milestone for Mega Infracon. With a built-up area of 30,000 sq. ft., the facility will be developed using advanced construction practices, ensuring durability, optimized workflow, and compliance with industry norms.

  • Designed specifically for FMCG manufacturing requirements
